# Gluecron Acceptable Use Policy

**Last updated: April 12, 2026**

## Overview

This Acceptable Use Policy ("AUP") applies to all users of Gluecron. Violation
of this policy may result in suspension or termination of your account.

## Prohibited Content

You may NOT use Gluecron to store, host, or distribute:

1. **Malware:** viruses, trojans, ransomware, spyware, or any malicious software
   designed to harm systems or steal data

2. **Stolen content:** code, data, or materials obtained through unauthorized
   access, hacking, or theft

3. **Illegal content:** materials that violate any applicable law, including
   but not limited to child exploitation material, content promoting terrorism,
   or materials violating export control laws

4. **Infringing content:** code or materials that violate copyright, trademark,
   patent, or other intellectual property rights of others

5. **Sensitive personal data:** Social Security numbers, credit card numbers,
   medical records, or other regulated personal data (except through
   purpose-built, encrypted features)

## Prohibited Activities

You may NOT use Gluecron to:

1. **Attack other systems:** launch DDoS attacks, port scanning, brute force
   attacks, or any unauthorized access attempts against third-party systems

2. **Mine cryptocurrency:** use our compute resources for cryptocurrency mining

3. **Send spam:** use the Service to send unsolicited bulk communications

4. **Circumvent security:** attempt to bypass authentication, rate limiting,
   or other security measures

5. **Abuse resources:** use excessive bandwidth, storage, or compute resources
   in a way that degrades service for other users

6. **Impersonate:** create accounts or repositories to impersonate other
   individuals or organizations

7. **Automate abusively:** run automated tools that place excessive load on
   the Service (reasonable CI/CD and API usage is permitted)

We will investigate reports promptly and take appropriate action.

## Enforcement

We may take any of the following actions in response to violations:
- Warning
- Temporary suspension of account or repository
- Permanent termination of account
- Removal of content
- Reporting to law enforcement (for illegal activity)

We strive to provide notice before taking action, except in cases of
serious or immediate threat.
